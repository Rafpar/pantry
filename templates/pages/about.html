Pantry is organized in three main lists. Base products, optional products and custom products
You can add product for each list separately. Just push „+ Add Product” button, specify product details and mark to which list product should be added.
After addition collapsed card will be displayed in respective list
You can expand detail of a product by tapping arrow at the bottom of a card
On the upper left corner current amount of a product is displayed, on the upper right corner there is red minus button to decrease current amount by 1. You can also edit details or delete product by pushing “Edit” button
When current amount of a product become lower than desired amount then current amount number on upper left corner is changing its color from green to red and product is then promoted to be visible in shopping list
You can display shopping list by pushing button “Shopping list”. Choose which list you would like to display by selecting check box. You need to choose at least one.
When you want to remove product from a list push "Done". Product will be removed from a list and its current amount will be updated with desired amount
This function is useful while shopping in the grocery.
By pressing user name on top right corner you will be redirected to user info page
Here you can get info about your account. You can also delete an account or change password.
When changing password apart new pasword you need to deliver email address used during signup
